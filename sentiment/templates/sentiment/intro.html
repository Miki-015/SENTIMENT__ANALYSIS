SENTIMENT ANALYSIS EXAMPLE
Imagine you received the following review on your Facebook page:
“I really enjoy how easy this product is to use and how it successfully helps my team complete their day-to-day tasks.”
Looking at this sentence, you can immediately tell the review is positive, But how would computer algorithms determine its sentiment? First of all, they would examine each word of the review and establish whether the mood they’re conveying is positive, negative, or neutral. Then, they would measure how many words there are in each category and, based on this number, assign a sentiment type. In the case of the mentioned review, there are two positively charged words – “enjoy” and “successfully” – which indicate that the entire sentence is positive.

Today’s advanced sentiment analysis tools can break down roughly any written message and determine its sentiment in no-time. As a result, you’re able to quickly learn the feelings and emotions behind your audience’s messages and respond appropriately.
